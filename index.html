Yes! We have extensive experience working with all major insurance carriers in Florida. Our team will document the damage, help you file your claim, and work directly with your insurance adjuster to ensure you receive fair compensation for necessary repairs or replacement.
For South Florida, we recommend metal roofing, concrete tile, or high-quality architectural shingles. Metal roofs offer excellent hurricane resistance and energy efficiency. Tile roofs provide outstanding durability in coastal environments. During your consultation, we'll help you choose the best option for your home and budget.
